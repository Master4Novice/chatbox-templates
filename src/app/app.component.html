Funda Of Life

Life is full of struggle and sometimes success. Less success and more struggle. Sometimes its interesting to be in problem but sometimes it feels like hell. There is no way out to escape life. We have to live it anyway. So, try to live life with fun and happiness. Life is only about taking oxygen in and getting out carbon dioxide. Just let the natural processess go on and stop thinking regarding anything that are around you, that's the only way to live life happily and stress-free.

The successful person is not happy with his success because he thinks that he deserves more success than he has achieved, and the one who is failure, he keeps on finding faults in himself or in others. We are actually not satisfied with ourselves. In any situation whether its success or failure we keep on finding problems. But actually we all are living in an illusion. Life is illusionary, one day we will die still we are running in a race, whether one is winning it or losing doesn't matter.
